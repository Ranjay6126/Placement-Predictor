About Our Project

What We Do

The Placement Prediction System is an AI-powered application designed to help students assess their placement prospects based on their academic and personal information.

Accurate Predictions

Our system uses a Random Forest Classifier with 77% accuracy to predict placement outcomes based on historical data patterns.

Personalized Insights

Get personalized insights about which factors most significantly impact your placement prospects.

Placement Assistant

Our AI-powered chatbot provides guidance on interview preparation, resume building, and placement processes.

Secure & Private

Your data is securely stored and only used for generating your placement predictions.
